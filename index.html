About Research Paper Toolbox

Constructing a research paper is an integral part of being a graduate student in the Information Architecture, Technical Communication, and Information Design programs at IIT.

There is no right or wrong way to put a paper together, but there are a wealth of tools available to make the process efficient and manageable.

If you are new to writing research papers, this course will guide you through each step. Even if you are experienced, you can refresh your knowledge with one or more lessons.
