Welcome to the Research Paper Toolbox

Constructing a research paper is an integral part of being a graduate student in the Information Architecture, Technical Communication and Information Design, and Technical Communication programs at IIT.

There is no right or wrong way to put a paper together, but there are a wealth of tools available to make the process efficient and manageable. This course will guide you through the basic steps of writing a research paper and introduce tools to help you along the way.

Whether you're new to research papers or just need a refresher, you can explore different lessons below. Click on a module to begin.
